Boiled Eggs

Boiled Eggs ingredients

This is a simple yet delicious recipe for boiled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 2 lt of water
  • salt

Cooking steps

  1. boil the water
  2. put the eggs in it and wait 10min
  3. peel the shells, pour some salt on top and enjoy
